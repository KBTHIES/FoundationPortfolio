Cube House

The Cube House Project, also known as the Wedding Pavillion design project, was a project where we were given a site covered in hedge, and it was up to us to modify the site to fit the program and the cube house itself.

The project had many pieces. We needed sections and a hand-rendered plan fit to a 1/4"=1' scale, as well as a scaled hand-rendered perspective. We also had digital renders, and finally, the scaled wooden model.

The design itself took heavy inspiration from a theatrical stage, and the layout had a counter-clockwise circulation pattern. Spaces were meant to emphasize transition and threshold.





Built by Kevin Thies 3/2017
